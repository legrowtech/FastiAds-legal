Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 21, 2026  ·  Last updated: February 21, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data FastiAds collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

1. Who We Are

FastiAds ("we", "our", "us") is a service operated by LeGrowTech that helps businesses create, manage, and launch advertising campaigns on Meta platforms (Facebook and Instagram).

Questions about this policy can be directed to: privacy@legrowtech.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Account information: name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Meta / Facebook data: when you connect your Meta account, we receive an OAuth access token, your Meta user ID, linked Page IDs, and Ad Account IDs necessary to create and manage campaigns.
  • Campaign data: ad copy, targeting parameters, budget settings, and campaign configurations you create in FastiAds.
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, timestamps, and browser/device information for analytics and debugging.
  • Communications: messages you send us via email or support channels.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate the FastiAds service.
  • Connect to Meta's Marketing API to create and manage your ad campaigns.
  • Generate AI-powered ad copy and targeting recommendations on your behalf.
  • Improve, debug, and enhance our platform.
  • Send service-related notifications and updates.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Meta Platform Data

FastiAds integrates with Meta's platform via the Facebook Login and Marketing API. By connecting your Meta account you authorise us to:

  • Read your Ad Accounts and Pages to populate your campaign settings.
  • Create and manage campaigns, ad sets, and ads on your behalf.

We do not sell or share your Meta data with third parties beyond what is necessary to operate the service. Our use of Meta platform data is subject to Meta's Platform Terms.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Service providers: infrastructure, analytics, and AI providers under confidentiality agreements.
  • Meta/Facebook: as required to operate campaigns through their API.
  • Legal authorities: when required by law, court order, or to protect our rights.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us at privacy@legrowtech.com.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted storage, secure HTTPS connections, and access controls. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Data portability.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@legrowtech.com.

9. Cookies

We use session cookies required for authentication and security. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

10. Children's Privacy

FastiAds is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of FastiAds after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
